Description:
  Simulates the sequencing reads from a FASTA file.

Usage:
  simulate_sequencing.sh  [OPTIONS]  -- FASTA_FILE

Options:
  -h|--help           Help message.
  -d|--outdir         Output directory.
  -l|--length         Length of the reads. Default: 30.
  -n|--num_reads      Number of reads. Default: 10.
         
Examples:
  simulate_sequencing.sh -- reference.fasta
