Description:
    Split a FASTA file.

Usage:
    fasta_split.sh  [options]  -- FASTA_FILE

Options:
    -h|--help           Help.
    -d|--outdir         Output directory. Default: $PWD.
    -n|--n_files        Number of files used. Default: 2.

Examples:
    fasta_split.sh -d out -n 4 -- input.fa

    NA
