Description:
    Samples a certain percentage of entries from a FASTA file.

Usage:
    fasta_sampling.sh  [options]  -- INPUT PERCENTAGE

Options:
    -h|--help           Help.
    -d|--outdir         Output directory. Default: $PWD.

Examples:
    fasta_sampling.sh -d out -- input.fa 50

Dependancies:
    perl
