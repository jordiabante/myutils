Description:
  Generates a random DNA or RNA sequence. 

Usage:
  random_sequence_generator.sh  [OPTIONS]

Options:
  -h|--help           Help message.
  -l|--length         Length of the sequence. Default: 10.
  -r|--rna            RNA bases. Default: DNA.
         
Examples:
   random_sequence_generator.sh -l 10
   random_sequence_generator.sh --rna -l 15
