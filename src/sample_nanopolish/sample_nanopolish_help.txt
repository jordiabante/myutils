Description:
  Samples a percentage of reads from a nanopolish output file. 

Usage:
  sample_nanopolish.sh  [OPTIONS]

Options:
  -h|--help           Help message.
  -p|--perc           Percentage of reads to be sampled. Default: 50.
         
Examples:
   sample_nanopolish.sh nano_ex.tsv
   sample_nanopolish.sh -l 50 nano_ex.tsv
