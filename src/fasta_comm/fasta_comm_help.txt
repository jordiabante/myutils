Description:
    Filters a FASTA_2 file based on the IDs of FASTA_1 file.

Usage:
    fasta_comm.sh  [options]  -- FASTA_1 FASTA_2

Options:
    -h|--help           Help.
    -d|--outdir         Output directory. Default: '$PWD'.
    --delim             Delimiter string. Default: '\S'.

Examples:
    fasta_comm.sh -d out -- input_1.fa input_2.fa

Dependancies:
    perl
