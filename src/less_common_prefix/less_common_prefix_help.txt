Description:
    Finds less common prefix between a set of strings.

Usage:
    less_common_prefix.sh  LIST

Options:
    -h--help                Help.

Examples:
    less_common_prefix.sh sample_A_1.txt sample_A_2.txt sample_B_1.txt sample_B_2.txt
